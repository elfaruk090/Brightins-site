Barka da zuwa BRIGHT TRADING INSTITUTE (BRIGHTINS):
Inda Ilimi da Nasara a Trading Ke Farawa

Gabatarwa:

BRIGHTINS makaranta ce ta musamman da aka kafa domin taimaka wa al’ummar Hausa wajen samun cikakken ilimi da kwarewa a financial markets. Burinmu shine ku sami damar fahimtar kasuwannin Forex, Crypto, Stocks da sauran su, kuma ku koyi yadda zaku iya amfani da su don canza rayuwarku da ta iyalanku cikin kwarin gwiwa.

Dalilin Da Ya Sa Zaku Zabi BRIGHTINS:

BRIGHTINS tana tafe da ƙwarewa da hangen nesa na Rabiu Adam Rabiu (Alaji Radar), wanda ya yi fice a fannin fundamental analysis. Da shawarwarinsa, mun kafa wannan makaranta domin kowa ya samu damar koyan dabaru da ilimin zamani a financial markets.

Muna da:
Basic Class: Kyauta ga kowa, domin ku fara daga tushe.
Advanced Class: na Naira 10,000 domin masu son zurfafa kwarewarsu a trading.
